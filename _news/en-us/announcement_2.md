On July 2, 2026, I had the pleasure of chairing a symposium at the [European Association for Social Psychology (EASP) congress](https://easp.eu/), titled **"Social Development: A Focus on Intergroup Cognition in Development"**, where I presented my own work on intergroup attitudes in preschool children in conflictual settings (*Intergroup attitudes in preschool children in conflictual intergroup settings: The case of Romania and Lebanon*).

Thanks to my colleagues who shared their research at this symposium:

- **Sophie Arnold** — *Teaching accurate genetics science reduces high schoolers' gender essentialism*
- **Nuccio Ludovico** — *Game Changers: Linking Motivational Aspects and Anti-Bullying Behavior in Youth Sports*

Thanks also to **Kristin Shutts**, who led the closing discussion and offered a broader perspective on these different approaches to social development and intergroup cognition in children and adolescents.
